Who We Are

Providing pay-as-you-go electricity plans for residents and businesses.

Midwest Light and Power is a leading energy supply and energy service company which offers pay-as-you-go electricity plans for residents and businesses.

As an electric company we are committed to providing 100% renewable, clean energy that is affordable, empowering householders and business leaders on their journeys to net zero.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Simpler, Cheaper, Reliable

Simpler

We make energy simpler – so we make your life easier. Our energy plans are simple, with the same great energy for everyone. And you can manage it all from your phone.

Cheaper

We make energy cheaper – so we help you save money. We invest in technology to reduce costs. And we pass savings on to our customers.

Reliable

We make energy reliable – so you don’t have to worry about sudden brownouts or blackouts. We provide 100% reliable electricity to all our customers as standard.

Making energy better, together

At Midwest, we put people first. Which means empowering our customers. Listening to them. Inviting them to build our company with us. We’re building an open energy company, with an engaged, supportive Community that takes centre stage.

Our Services

How We Serve You

Keeping on top of your energy

Life can be complicated but your energy doesn’t need to be. Your Midwest Account makes it easy for you to keep on top of your energy. Sign in online, or download the app. You can track your usage, submit meter readings and check your balance.

And it’s not just about making things easier. Understanding what you use and how much it costs helps you spot opportunities to save. Especially if you’ve got a prepaid meter and we’re able to help you manage the energy you use in the app.
