Getting Started

Yes, probably. Midwest provides pay-as-you-go electricity in most areas. It’s easy to find out if it’s available to you. Just enter your address or zip code in our address look-up tool and see if Midwest can service you. Then, you can enroll online in under 2 minutes. If you have trouble, give Midwest a call and a representative will help or connect with us over chat.

We keep it simple. All we ask for is your name, physical address, home information, email and mobile number (that’s how we keep you updated and send balance alerts). We want your birthday (to send you Happy Birthday greetings) and you will have to create a password for future account access, but that’s it.

Sign up and charge your account with at least ₦10,000 before 5 PM and we’ll switch you to the Midwest network on the same-day from Monday through Saturday. Switch to Midwest to get started.

You can start your electric service with Midwest with as little as ₦10,000 if you already have a prepaid meter. Because we have no start-up fees, 100% of your initial charge-up goes directly to your energy account. This is not a deposit—it is your money, used to pay for your electricity as you go. If you don’t have a prepaid meter, you’ll need to purchase one, but not to worry— we can work out a convenient and affordable plan for you.

After you enroll and charge up your account with at least ₦10,000 to activate service with Midwest, we should have you up and running the same day.

Yes, we encourage you to switch. We’re confident we can save you money and provide hassle-free electricity, so switching to Midwest is easy and straightforward.

It should not cost you a thing to switch to Midwest. If you have an outstanding debt with your current provider, you’ll likely need to pay that. You may also want to check if they have an early termination fee.

We got you covered. Just make the switch and we’ll take care of notifying your current provider that you've made the switch to Midwest. There should be no service interruption whatsoever.

Midwest is the electricity that moves with you. As long as the new place is in our service area, you’re all good – and you keep your current balance through the move. To transfer your service, chat or call us at 0800-MIDWEST. All we need is the new address, the date for stopping service at the old place and the date for beginning service at the new place. Simple, right?!

Yes. During switching, you will have the option to start service immediately or pick a future date to coincide with a move or the end of a contract. All you have to do is charge up your account with at least ₦10,000 after you enroll. Helpful tip: if you’re moving into a new home or apartment, you may want to schedule your power to turn on a day early.

Midwest provides electricity without a monthly bill – you pay in advance for electricity as you go. However, sometimes you need proof that you have electricity with us, or you want to see your payment history and usage. You can download a copy from your Account menu under Usage History.

Welcome to Midwest and a better way to consume your electricity – finally! Pay-as-you-go energy with Midwest works a little differently from postpaid electricity services. With a postpaid account, you can expect ridiculous monthly bills and a no-nonsense demand to pay. Most postpaid energy plans have late payment fees, disconnect fees, early termination charges, and other hidden fees. So we’ve got your back and will help you save money while you save the world with 100% clean, renewable energy – just like the superhero you are!

As a Midwest customer, you get the benefits of no deposit, no contracts, no surprises, no hassles and no hidden fees. Choose how you want to power up your account. Autopay takes the worry and stress out of recharging your energy account. If autopay isn’t right for you, choose when and how much you recharge as you go. Either way, with only ₦10,000, you can get the power on today. Midwest sends you a text to tell you how much electricity you’re using each day, or you can check your account dashboard.

We understand that things don’t always go as planned. If you run out of credit, you can always use your Emergency credit of up to ₦30,000. If you need additional help, call us at 0812 778 4545 or email customer.support@midwest.com during our business hours and we’ll do our best to help.

Helpful tip: If you need to use emergency credit, you will have to repay the amount used when you next top up. For example, If you use ₦5,000 of emergency or credit, your meter will show ₦5,000 owed. If you top up ₦10,000 next time, the ₦5,000 will be taken off, leaving you ₦5,000 of normal credit.

In the event of an electricity-related emergency, such as a power outage, or in the event of problems call us on our emergency line 643.

There are no surprise bills at the end of the month with Midwest. In fact, there are no bills at all. You can always check your current service balance and estimated number of days of power remaining on your dashboard.

Midwest provides you with SMS keywords that help you get the information you want easily and quickly. Text our short code to 643 to get started. Here’s the list of keywords and descriptions:

  • BAL – balance request
  • LAST – last recharge amount
  • EMCR – request an emergency credit
  • OUTAGE – information on reporting a power outage
  • BARCODE – sends you your barcode
  • RECHARGE – options on how to recharge your account
  • ASSIST – provides info to auto-response SMS keywords
  • MOVE – info on how to take Midwest with you when you move
  • HOLIDAY – explanation of how things work on a holiday
  • START – subscribe to SMS notification
  • STOP – unsubscribe from marketing SMS notifications

Billing and Payment

We understand that things don’t always go as planned. If you run out of credit, you can always use your Emergency credit of up to ₦30,000. If you need additional help, call us at 0812 778 4545 or email customer.support@midwest.com during our business hours and we’ll do our best to help.

Helpful tip: If you need to use emergency credit, you will have to repay the amount used when you next top up.

There are several options to choose from to add funds to your Midwest account:
• Pay online with a debit or credit card, 24 x 7 x 365
• Recharge with Paystack or Remita
• Add funds with OPay and Kuda
• Make a cash deposit at one of our cash payment locations
• Visit an authorized payment location near you (these typically charge a cash handling fee)

We don’t charge a fee if you pay late, as long as you pay before service is disconnected. It’s our privilege to have you as a customer, and we hate fees as much as you do.

Yes. We’re always happy to receive a payment and restore your power. You only have five days to make a payment after your service is suspended. After five days, your service will be disconnected.

If your account balance goes negative and your power is disconnected, there is a ₦12,500 reconnect fee. You will need to pay the reconnect fee and recharge your account to bring your balance above zero. If you need help paying the reconnect fee, we can draw up a personalised payment plan so that you can pay it slowly over time.

We do not charge a fees other than transaction fees and standing charges. This is because we are required to charge for banking transactions as well as the cost of meter installation and maintenance and maintaining the electricity distribution network.

Solar BuyBack Program

You’ll receive these payments as a top-up and they will appear on your next statement as ‘BBCK payment’. The maximum top-up amount is ₦200,000.

If you are a Solar BuyBack customer with a smart meter installed, press the left blue button on your smart meter 7 times until you see A- in the top right-hand side of the screen. This will display the kwh of energy you’ve exported.
