Sell your solar output to us

Trade electricity with us

As a customer of Midwest, if you have solar panels on your home, we use your two-way electricity meter and are able to buy back any overproduction that your panels produce.

We then sell that excess power, in real time, at the current market price. At the end of the month, we will send you payment for the energy sold back to the grid or you can leave it in your account to use toward future electricity use. It’s that simple. Just fill out our application form to sign up.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a power outage?

You can report an outage directly through our mobile app, by logging into your account on our website, or by calling our 24/7 emergency hotline at 643. Our teams are on standby to restore power safely and quickly.

How does the Solar Buyback program work?

If you have solar panels installed, our bidirectional meters track the excess energy you generate. We credit this surplus energy to your account at the current market rate, which can be used to offset your future bills or cashed out at the end of the month.

What are the Peak and Off-peak periods?

To help you save, our tariffs vary by time of day. Peak period is 5 PM to 10 PM. Shoulder period is 8 AM to 5 PM. Off-peak period is 10 PM to 8 AM. Running high-energy appliances during off-peak hours gives you the lowest rates.

How can I pay my electricity bill?

We offer several convenient payment methods. You can pay via the Midwest App, our website portal using a debit card, via USSD code *MID# (*643#), or at any authorized walk-in payment center.

How do I switch my tariff plan?

Switching is easy! Log in to your dashboard, select "Manage Plan," and choose the available tariff that suits your lifestyle (Standard, SuperSave, AlwaysOn or EVolve). Changes typically take effect within 24 hours.

What should I do if my prepaid meter rejects a token?

First, ensure there is power supply in your area. If the problem persists, check for error codes on the meter screen and consult the manual. If you see "Error 04" or similar, please contact our support team via chat or phone for a reset code.
