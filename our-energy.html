Where does your power come from?

We are proud to supply 100% renewable electricity. We don't just buy green certificates; we invest in direct power purchase agreements with local generators.

Solar Power

Sourced from small and large-scale solar farms across the region, capturing clean energy from the sun.

Wind Energy

Harnessing the breeze from both onshore and offshore wind farms to provide consistent baseload power.

Hydro Electric

Reliable, flow-based energy generation ensuring that when the sun sets, your lights stay on.
