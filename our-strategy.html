Building a Decentralized Grid

The old way of generating electricity involves massive, dirty coal plants far away from where people live. Our strategy flips this on its head.

We are building a decentralized grid where homes and businesses generate their own power through solar, store it in batteries, and trade it back to the grid. We call this the "Energy Internet".

Key Strategic Pillars:
  • Digital First: App-based management for total control.
  • Customer Empowerment: Helping you use less, not more.
  • Local Generation: Supporting micro-grids in communities.
