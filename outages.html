Stay Safe During an Outage

While we work to restore power, please keep these tips in mind.

Unplug Appliances

Prevent damage from power surges when electricity is restored.

Keep Freezers Closed

A full freezer will hold temperature for about 48 hours if the door remains closed.

Use Flashlights

Avoid candles to reduce fire risk. Use battery-powered lights.

Check Updates

Use the Midwest App or check this page for restoration estimates.
