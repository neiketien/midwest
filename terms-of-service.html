1. Supply of Electricity

We agree to supply electricity to your premises via the local distribution network. Service quality is subject to grid stability.

2. Payments

All services are pay-as-you-go. Accounts must remain in credit to ensure continuous supply. Emergency credit must be repaid upon the next top-up.

3. Access to Equipment

You must allow our authorized technicians access to your property for meter installation, maintenance, or safety inspections.

4. Termination

You may switch providers at any time without penalty, provided any outstanding debts (including emergency credit) are cleared.
